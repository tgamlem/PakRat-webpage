Features

This screenshot shows the basic user interface of PakRat's 'Loan' feature.

This screenshot shows the layout of a completed form in "My Paks.""

Users will be able to easily create a new Pak, fill out a form for each of their items, snap a quick picture, and view everything within their collection. More popular Paks such as movies, coins, and books will have specialized forms, but if your Pak is a unique niche, you can also customize your own form!

For those who like to share their Pak with others, Pakrat will have a 'Loan' feature that will allow users to track which items are on loan, who they loaned it to, and how long it's been out.

Collectors know that their Pak can never truly be complete. That's why PakRat will offer a 'Wishlist' to allow users to catalog items that they'd like to one day get ahold of. This Wishlist can be easily shared with others, so the whole world can know about the perfect thing to buy for a fellow Pakrat.
